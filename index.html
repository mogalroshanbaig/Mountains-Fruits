Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions regarding product dosage, storage, and marketplace delivery.

Take 1 teaspoon (approx. 3 g) of Mudavattukal Kilangu powder and mix it with warm water, warm milk, or a clear herbal broth. Consume once daily or as directed by a healthcare professional.
Our powder is made strictly from the purple outer peel (rind) of the mangosteen fruit. The rind contains a highly concentrated group of plant-based antioxidants called Xanthones, which are not present in the white fruit pulp.
Our packages are dispatched from Bangalore. We are officially registered on Amazon, Flipkart, and Swiggy Instamart, allowing you to order across India and receive express shipping in metropolitan areas.
Store in a cool, dry place. Keep away from moisture and direct sunlight. Ensure you reseal the pack zipper immediately after use to protect the biological elements.
